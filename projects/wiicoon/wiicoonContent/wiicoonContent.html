WIICOON PROJECT

Wiicoon was a project completely made it by Luis Fernando Perez Salcedo and me, at that time we were just engineering graduates so we started a project/company and learned a lot of things in the middle of the way.

The objective was made an electronic device which allows control home light with smartphones sounds very simple at the beginning, but was a long trip, sometimes funny, sometimes not so much.

Hardware design was my responsibility, we wanted to make a very small device because the idea was put it into the electrical socket and reconnect there the electrical wires. you can see some of the original drawings here.

The Microcontroller program was made by Luis Fernando, who started to know better the esp8266 microcontroller than his family. (joke) note: we never use git tools in whole project so versions, controls were a headache and probably we lost some interesting version and updates. Anyway, here you can find one of the ultimates versions of the ESP8266 code.
